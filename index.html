About Floatlands

Floatlands is a surreal 2D platformer set in a dreamlike world. Pedro wakes in a reality he believes to be true, until his friend Sue is kidnapped. To save her, he must journey through floating dream realms filled with traps, secrets, and choices that decide the ending.

Created for EXE 2025, Floatlands is just the beginning of Team Kairo's journey.
